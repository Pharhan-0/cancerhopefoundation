We are dedicated to supporting individuals affected by cancer, offering financial and emotional assistance, and advancing research efforts toward a cure.

Our Impact

Our Programs

Financial Assistance

We offer financial support to individuals and families facing economic hardship due to cancer. This includes assistance with medical bills, travel expenses, and more.

Our counseling and support groups provide a safe space for patients and their families to share their experiences, seek advice, and find comfort.

About Cancer

Cancer is a major public health issue worldwide. It occurs when abnormal cells in the body grow uncontrollably, often spreading to other parts of the body. There are over 100 different types of cancer, affecting various organs and tissues. The most common forms include breast cancer, lung cancer, prostate cancer, and colorectal cancer.

While advances in medical technology have improved the survival rates for many cancers, early detection and proper treatment are critical to improve outcomes. Prevention, through lifestyle changes such as quitting smoking, reducing alcohol consumption, and maintaining a healthy diet, can also reduce the risk of cancer significantly.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), cancer accounted for approximately 10 million deaths globally in 2020, and this number continues to rise each year.
